Website Title:
Personal Portfolio Website

Problem:
Students and beginners often lack a professional online presence to showcase their skills, projects, and achievements in one place.

Idea:
Create a personal portfolio website using HTML and CSS to display my profile, skills, projects, and contact information in a clean and
visually appealing way.

Features:
- Home section with introduction
- About Me section
- Skills showcase
- Projects section with descriptions
- Contact form or contact details
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop

First Webpage Plan:
The first webpage will be the homepage. It will include a navigation bar, a brief introduction about me (Aritra Ghosh), a profile image,
and links to other sections like About, Projects, and Contact. It will be simple, clean, and easy to navigate.
